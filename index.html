MV Agusta F4CC:

Power Output: The engine produces a maximum power output of 200 horsepower (147 kW) at 12,200 rpm. 

Torque: It offers a peak torque of 115 Nm (85 lb-ft) at 8,000 rpm. 

Transmission: The motorcycle features a 6-speed constant mesh gearbox with a multi-plate wet clutch. 

Frame: The F4CC comes with a tubular steel trellis frame and aluminum alloy single-sided swingarm. 

Suspension: It is equipped with fully adjustable √ñhlins suspension, including a USD telescopic fork at the front and a monoshock at the rear. 

Brakes: The motorcycle features a high-performance braking system with Brembo radial calipers and dual 320 mm discs at the front, and a single 210 mm disc with a Nissin caliper at the rear. 

Wheels: The F4CC rides on lightweight forged aluminum alloy wheels. 

Weight: The motorcycle has a dry weight of 187 kg (412 lbs). 

Limited Edition: The F4CC is a limited edition model with only 100 units produced.

Electronics: It includes advanced electronics such as ride-by-wire throttle, multiple riding modes, traction control, and ABS. 

 Energica Ego 45 Limited Editon :

 Kawasaki Ninja H2R : 

Engine: The Ninja H2R is powered by a liquid-cooled, supercharged 998cc inline-four engine. It features a unique supercharger system developed by Kawasaki called the "Supercharger System." This system provides significant power gains over a naturally aspirated engine. 

Power: The Ninja H2R is capable of producing an astonishing amount of power. The engine generates a maximum power output of around 310 horsepower (230 kW) at the crankshaft. With ram air effect, the power output can be further enhanced. 

Torque: The motorcycle delivers a high amount of torque, providing exceptional acceleration. The maximum torque output is around 156 Nm (115 lb-ft) at the crankshaft. 

Transmission: It comes equipped with a 6-speed cassette-type gearbox, enabling precise and quick gear shifts. 

Frame: The Ninja H2R features a lightweight and highly rigid trellis frame, providing excellent stability and handling at high speeds. 

Suspension: It is equipped with top-of-the-line suspension components. The front suspension consists of a fully adjustable inverted fork, while the rear features a fully adjustable Uni-Trak monoshock. 

Brakes: The Ninja H2R comes with a high-performance braking system. It includes dual semi-floating 330mm Brembo discs with radial-mount 4-piston calipers at the front and a single 250mm disc with a 2-piston caliper at the rear. 

Aerodynamics: The motorcycle incorporates aerodynamic features to enhance its performance. It includes winglets on the fairing, designed to improve stability and downforce at high speeds. 

Electronics: The Ninja H2R is equipped with advanced electronics to ensure precise control and rider safety. It features multiple riding modes, traction control, launch control, quick shifter, and Kawasaki's Intelligent Anti-Lock Braking System (KIBS). 

Weight: The Ninja H2R has a dry weight of around 216 kilograms (476 pounds). It is designed to be as lightweight as possible to maximize its power-to-weight ratio.
